About Mangaluru (Kudla)

A coastal paradise with rich heritage

Mangaluru, affectionately called "Kudla" in the local Tulu language, is a vibrant coastal city in Karnataka, India. Known for its pristine beaches, historic temples, and rich cultural tapestry, Mangaluru offers an unforgettable experience for every traveler.

From the serene shores of Panambur Beach to the architectural marvels of ancient temples, from the aromatic local cuisine to the warm hospitality of its people, Mangaluru is a destination that captivates hearts and creates lasting memories.

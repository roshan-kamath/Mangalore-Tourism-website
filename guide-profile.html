About Me

Hello! I'm Ravi Shetty, a passionate food blogger and cultural enthusiast born and raised in Mangaluru. For over 10 years, I've been exploring and documenting the rich culinary heritage of Kudla, from traditional Mangalorean dishes to contemporary fusion cuisine.

My journey started as a hobby during my college days, photographing local eateries and street food vendors. Today, I conduct specialized food tours that take visitors through the aromatic lanes of Mangaluru, introducing them to authentic flavors and the stories behind each dish.

Beyond food, I'm deeply interested in our local culture, traditions, and the history that makes Mangaluru unique. Whether it's explaining the significance of a temple festival or sharing folklore associated with a local dish, I love bringing our heritage to life for visitors.

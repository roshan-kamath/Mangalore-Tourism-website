About Namma Kudla

Connecting travelers with the heart and soul of Mangaluru

Who We Are

Namma Kudla is a modern travel blog dedicated to showcasing the beauty, culture, and heritage of Mangaluru (affectionately known as "Kudla" in the local Tulu language). Founded by passionate locals and travel enthusiasts, our mission is to inspire travelers with authentic stories, practical tips, and engaging guides that capture the true essence of this coastal paradise.

From serene beaches and historic temples to vibrant cuisine and hidden gems, we aim to connect visitors with the soul of Mangaluru. Whether you're a first-time traveler planning your trip, a food blogger seeking culinary adventures, or a local guide wanting to share your expertise, this platform is designed for you.

Our Story

Namma Kudla was born from a simple observation: while Mangaluru is rich in culture, heritage, and natural beauty, many travelers were missing out on authentic local experiences. Tourist guides often covered only the mainstream attractions, leaving countless hidden gems undiscovered.

In 2024, a group of local enthusiasts came together with a vision‚Äîto create a comprehensive platform that would bridge the gap between travelers and the authentic Mangaluru experience. We started by documenting lesser-known beaches, family-run eateries serving traditional cuisine, and ancient temples with fascinating histories.

Today, Namma Kudla has grown into a thriving community of travelers, bloggers, photographers, and local guides, all united by their love for Mangaluru. Our platform features hundreds of articles, thousands of photos, and connections to expert local guides who bring the city's stories to life.

What Makes Mangaluru Special

Mangaluru, located on the southwestern coast of Karnataka, is a city where ancient traditions meet modern aspirations. Known for its:

  • Pristine Beaches: From the bustling Panambur to the serene Tannirbhavi, our coastline offers something for every beach lover.
  • Culinary Heritage: Famous for spicy seafood, traditional Mangalorean dishes, and unique coastal flavors that tantalize taste buds.
  • Cultural Diversity: A harmonious blend of various communities, religions, and traditions living together for centuries.
  • Historical Significance: Ancient temples, colonial churches, and historical landmarks that tell stories of our rich past.
  • Natural Beauty: Lush green landscapes, scenic backwaters, and the majestic Western Ghats in proximity.
  • Warm Hospitality: The genuine warmth and friendliness of Kudla's people make every visitor feel at home.
